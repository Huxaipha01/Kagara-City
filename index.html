ABOUT

Kagara is a town in Niger State, Nigeria, and the proud capital of the Rafi Local Government Area. Its motto is "Land of Peace and Tourism," and its postal code is 922. Kagara's rich history is evident in its many accomplished residents, including prominent figures such as Dr. Abubakar Imam (Kagara), author of the book ‘Magana Jarice’, Sa'idu Madaki (Masoyi), and Abdul-Kareem Lafene.

The Kagara Emirate was founded by the Nagwamatse of Kontagora through Madaki Masoyi during the reign of the first Emir, Ahmadu Attahiru. Kagara Emirate Established after the Dasuki local government reforms of 1976, Kagara Emirate is part of the Niger East Senatorial District Zone (B).
